https://github.com/rwaldron/tc39-notes/blob/master/es6/2014-07/instantiation-reform.pdf

AWB: Currently:

new Foo(arg)
  1. Foo[[Construct]](arg) ::=
  2. let obj = Foo[@@create]()
  3. foo.call(obj, arg)
